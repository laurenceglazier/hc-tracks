Add more tracks:
  1. Upload .mp3 files into /audio/ in this repo.
  2. Make a new list item that links back to the app with a ?track= parameter.
    Format: https://hybridclassical.ai/?track=encodeURIComponent(fileURL)
    Example for audio/my_piece.mp3:
    https://hybridclassical.ai/?track=https%3A%2F%2Flaurenceglazier.github.io%2Fhc-tracks%2Faudio%2Fmy_piece.mp3
